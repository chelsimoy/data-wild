Gun Traces in Chicago

Between 2001 and 2012, Chicago law enforcement confiscated nearly 50,000 guns used to commit crimes in the Windy City and which were registered in other states. These guns found their way to Chicago from every state in the United States. Gun tracing is where law enforcement track the movement of crime guns.
